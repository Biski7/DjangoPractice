This is a movie and tv series review site. It consists of two sections, one for the movies and another for tv series. You can browse movies individually and add them to the cart. You can browse the movies and tv series as per their Genres. For this purpose you can click on the movies or tvseries option in the Navbar. The movies and tv series have additional information including imdb rating, rotten tomatoes rating, description and the cast of the movie or tv series. 
You can create an account from the home page and then book tickets by adding them to cart and proceed to checkout.
Further enhancement includes the rating that can be given by the user, writing reviews by the user, forget password option and order form sent to the user's email. I am currently working on adding these functions.
